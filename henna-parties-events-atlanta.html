Henna for Parties & Events in Atlanta

Unforgettable Entertainment Your Guests Will Love

Looking for something unique and memorable for your next event? Hiral's Henna provides professional henna entertainment for parties and events of all kinds across Atlanta, Gainesville, and the greater North Georgia area. Henna is the perfect interactive activity — guests of all ages enjoy choosing their own designs and walking away with beautiful, temporary body art they'll show off for days. It's entertainment and a party favor in one.

Birthday Parties

Whether it's a child's birthday, a sweet sixteen, or an adult milestone celebration, henna is a crowd-pleasing addition to any birthday party. Kids love picking out fun designs like flowers, butterflies, and stars, while teens and adults enjoy more detailed, intricate patterns. Hiral's Henna brings a curated design menu so every guest can choose a style that fits them. We work efficiently with groups so the party keeps moving, and every guest gets a chance to be decorated. It's a creative, screen-free activity that kids and adults genuinely enjoy.

Baby Showers

Celebrate the mom-to-be with beautiful henna at her baby shower. Guests love having delicate henna designs applied during the party, and many expecting mothers choose to have special belly henna art — a stunning addition to maternity photos. Our fresh, organic henna paste is made with only three natural ingredients and is completely safe for expecting mothers and guests with sensitive skin. Baby shower henna adds an elegant, interactive touch that makes the celebration feel truly special.

Bachelorette Parties & Bridal Showers

Give the bride-to-be and her crew a memorable experience with henna at the bachelorette party or bridal shower. It's the perfect activity for a relaxed gathering — guests can sip drinks and socialize while getting gorgeous henna designs. Many brides use this as an opportunity to try out henna styles before their wedding day, while bridesmaids and friends enjoy temporary henna tattoos they'll love showing off. Available at homes, hotels, Airbnbs, or event venues anywhere in our service area.

Corporate Events & Team Building

Henna is an inclusive, engaging, and conversation-starting activity that works beautifully at corporate events. Beyond the events listed above, Hiral's Henna provides henna for engagement parties, graduation celebrations, anniversary dinners, Eid and Diwali parties, Navratri events, photo shoots, gender reveal parties, retirement celebrations, and any occasion where you want to offer your guests something unique and beautiful. How Event Henna Works

Booking Hiral's Henna for your event is simple. Let us know your event type, the estimated number of guests, your date, and your location. We'll recommend a package based on how many guests you'd like served and how long you'd like us there. On the day of, we arrive early, set up, and begin creating beautiful designs for your guests using fresh, organic, homemade henna paste. Guests choose from a design menu or request custom patterns, and each design typically takes 5 to 15 minutes depending on size and complexity. We handle everything — all you have to do is enjoy the party.
